Hi! You want your ideas turned to reality?

Idreality is all you need. You've just gotten all you need at one stop.

Idreality is an innovation and life coaching hub, a digital platform that provides solutions to the ideas that have not been birthed, taking them from incubation to execution. It is a free online space to accommodate the youth and their ideas, and walk them towards realization of their potential.

Who Are iDreality Participants?

Ideator

This user joins the forum and shares daily problems faced at job, home, or anywhere. The user helps the Dorks to innovate faster, smarter, and easier

Dork

This user is a techie. Dork connotes an expert or enthusiast obsessed with a hobby in programming. The user eats and breathes code

Aid

These are the users of development assistance to iDreality. They help in the development of iDreality site. They also help in handy with donations

Financier

This user is a sponsor. The user introduces and supports a proposal for implementation. The user supports the Ideator and the Dork financially to actualize their project.
